Watches And More To Celebrate The Return Of Formula 1

Netflix's Drive to Survive season five was released this past weekend and the Bahrain GP is only days away. This is the magical time for any Formula 1 fan, when no points have been scored and nothing is out of reach yet. Soon we will get the answers to how the teams improved their cars in the off-season, how the new drivers are going to perform, and whether anyone beat Max Verstappen and Red Bull.


For the uninitiated out there, the ties between F1 and timepieces go back to the beginning of the sport – originally with cockpit chronographs and stopwatches that would time laps, and then wristwatches developed for the same reason. This natural combination fueled by a need for accurate timekeeping created a partnership that led to sponsorships.
